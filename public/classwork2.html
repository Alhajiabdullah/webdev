Angela's Recipe For The Best Cinamon Rolls

Ingredients

for the dough:

  • 3/4 cup warm milk
  • 2 1/2 teaspoon yeasts
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flour

For the filling:

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons ground cinamon
  • 1/4 cup butter

Instructions

  1. mix the milk with the yeast, sugar, eggs
  2. melt butter and add to the mixture
  3. add the flour and mix till it combines to a dough
  4. knead the dough for 10 minuites
  5. transfer the dough into a large bowl and cover with plastic wrap. leave it somewhere to ris for 2 hours
  6. after the dough has doubled in size, roll it out into a large rectangle
